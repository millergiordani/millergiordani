Things to Do in Ann Arbor

We're so glad that you'll be joining us to celebrate this November! We encourage you to take advantage of all of the wonderful sights and scenes Ann Arbor has to offer.

In addition to the recommended activities below, we have also organized a few special activities for out-of-town guests. Sign up here!

An Afternoon on Main Street

Spend a day in the life of Lindsay & James! This is a typical afternoon on Main Street for us. Enjoy!

  1. Try all the samples at Cherry Republic
  2. Explore games & comics at Vault of Midnight
  3. Pick up a new book at Crazy Wisdom
  4. Buy a snarky greeting card from Rock, Paper, Scissors
  5. Recharge with a charcuterie plate from The Raven’s Club

More Fun Things to Do in Ann Arbor

  • Catch a movie at the historic Michigan Theater
  • Visit the shops & stores in Kerrytown
  • Go to Zingerman's Deli or Zingerman's Roadhouse for delicious food
  • Get your Michigan swag at MDen
  • Visit Farmer's Market on Saturday or the Artisan Market on Sunday
  • Take a class at Zingerman's Bakehouse
  • Catch a Jazz show at the Blue Llama
  • Laugh your butt off at an improv show at Pointless Brewery and Theatre

Great For Families

  • Learn and play at the Hands On Museum
  • Play some arcade games at Pinball Pete's
  • Visit The Petting Farm at Domino Farms
  • Explore the University of Michigan Museum of Natural History
  • Compare the 5 playgrounds at Burns Park Elementary School

Get Outta Town

  • Visit Historic Greenfield Village or the Henry Ford Museum in Dearborn
  • Visit the Dexter Cider Mill - check to make sure they are still open for the season!
  • Explore shops & stores in Depot Town in Ypsilanti
  • Take a tour of the Jiffy Mix factory in Chelsea (Mon-Fri only)

Our Favorite Spots for Food & Beverage

It's really hard to go wrong when eating out in Ann Arbor. These are just a few of our favorites, but you will find A LOT of incredible places to eat and drink.

  • Biercamp - For brats, beers, and delectable spreads
  • Seva - For delicious vegetarian and health-conscious cuisine
  • Zingerman's Roadhouse - For the most comforting, satisfying meal you'll ever have
  • Cardamom - For Indian fusion food, satisfying and healthful
  • Chela's - For quick, tasty mexican street food
  • Frita Batido's - For cuban fusion cuisine unlike anything you've ever had
  • The Raven’s Club - For appetizers, cocktails, and homemade ginger beer
  • Fresh Forage - For healthy, local, farm-to-table and foraged foods
  • Good Time Charley's - For the true "college" experience
  • HOMES Brewery - For creative beers and korean fusion snacks
  • Pileated Brewing Company - For local beer flights
  • Last Word - For the speakeasy vibe and craft cocktails
  • Isalita - For tequila flights, great for first dates ;)
